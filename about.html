About METRO JOBS

Your trusted partner in recruitment and HR solutions

Corporate Profile

Metro Jobs & Payment Solutions Inc. is a company of highly energetic, talented and dedicated individuals committed to serving companies in need of human resource and manpower assistance. The company was registered at SEC on August 29, 2007.

It is owned and managed by a group of experienced human resource professionals. We realized in the past that in spite of the existence of numerous recruitment agencies in the Philippines, there was a need for professionally managed recruitment agency and that the need was urgent. It is this very concern that brought Metro Jobs into existence.

We provide staffing services for various companies across industries such as Manufacturing, Telecommunications, Information Technology, Tourism Industry, Clothing, Retail, Food Service, Legal and Healthcare industries.

Our tagline "Placing The Right People In The Right Jobs" drives us to ensure that the manpower we provide are the most qualified and are able to contribute to a company's productivity and growth.

Mission & Vision

Mission

  • To provide employment to a greater number of people needing jobs.
  • To boost clients' efficiency and help increase their profitability through quality and cost-effective services.

Vision

To become the preferred and premier provider of a whole range of staffing and human resource requirements of companies in the country.

Our Corporate Values

Core Philosophy

To create and establish a corporate climate of trust and authenticity with our clients and the people who form our work force. It's an ideal blend of "Service and Concern." We forge mutually beneficial partnerships with our clients, protecting their interests and sharing knowledge and expertise in Human Resources.

Service Commitment

We bring our commitment to a fruitful completion, which leads to long lasting client relationships. We match the skills and competencies required by client companies through careful selection, screening and processing of candidates to obtain appropriate and responsive results for full client satisfaction.
